HK ASSOCIATES is the award winning
architecture practice of husband and wife
team Kathy Hancox and Michael Kothke.

Originally from Canada, Kathy and Michael's
expertise is built upon a lineage that runs
through internationally renowned
architecture firms in Vancouver, British
Columbia and San Francisco, California.

In 2003, an opportunity to work together with
Rick Joy brought them to Arizona where they
fell in love with the desert, later
establishing HK ASSOCIATES.
